Details

Over the past 10 years we have created a niche by providing large hospitals with warranty replacement plans and schedules. We provide the equipment and the personnel to make sure that costly out of warranty equipment is replaced in a timely manner.

We use individually created scripts and procedures to make sure every swap is done with no data loss and with 100% customer satisfaction.
